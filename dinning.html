DINING AT LAILA'S COUNTY - CUCINA

Overview

A family restaurant that serves you delicious food ranging from western cuisine such as the Ceasar's salad and pastas' to Indian cuisine, from the north and the south, to chinese cuisine. It opens from 7:30 till 10:30 to bring you either a continental breakfast of bread and omellette or a regional breakfast that has idly, dosa, poori,etc...
Cucina is open from 11:00 am to 11:00 pm for lunch and dinner where you can eat sumptuous meals that will definitely satisfy you. You can also order some liquor from the bar in the evening.
Trivia for non-Italians: Cucina means kitchen in Italian.

THE DRAGON LOUNGE

Overview

A popular rooftop lounge of Pondicherry that opens in the evening grants an astonishing night view along with a big screen projection of sports of the season. Liquor ranges from beer to vodkas to scotch and all different varieties. Some notable liquors are Budeweiser beer, Belvedere vodka, Beefeater gin, Cointreau liqueur, Black dog scotch, Hennessey cognac. You can also order cocktails alcoloholic and non-alcoholic. Fresh juice is also available.
You can also find food types from all over the world and of course a tandoor oven right on the rooftop brings you fresh tandoori chicken and all tandoor made food. It just has everything you need to comfortably drink your alcohol.
